The Engineer

Akilesh Yelchuru is an aerospace engineering student focused on turning technical curiosity into mission-ready systems. His work spans rocketry, robotics, CAD, simulation, controls, and systems engineering documentation.

Across Panther Rocket Lab, NASA L’SPACE, robotics projects, and independent simulation tools, he works from concept to analysis to iteration, using engineering judgment to connect design decisions with performance, risk, and manufacturability.

His goal is to build aerospace systems that are technically rigorous, clearly documented, and shaped by the realities of testing, constraints, and mission impact.
